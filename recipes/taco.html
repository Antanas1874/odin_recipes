Taco Recipe

This is a meatless taco in a corn tortilla. Simple and delicious. You will want seconds or thirds. Shhh.

  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Stir in the onion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned golden brown, about 10 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes, stirring until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Stir in the chicken broth, Swiss chard, and salt. Cover and reduce heat to low.
  4. Simmer until chard is nearly tender, about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove lid and increase heat to medium, stirring until the liquid evaporates, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
